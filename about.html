About Me

I am passionate about turning data into meaningful stories that drive business decisions. My career journey began in web development, where I worked as a freelance developer building and optimizing websites for various clients. While working on these projects, I discovered how powerful data could be in understanding user behavior and improving digital experiences. This curiosity led me to transition into the data profession, where I could focus on extracting insights and building data-driven solutions. I later worked with an Ad Tech startup, where I supported data acquisition by extracting website data and integrating it into CRM platforms to enhance customer engagement and marketing intelligence. Since then, I have continued to grow my expertise in designing scalable data solutions that empower organizations to make informed decisions.
I played a key role in designing and delivering robust data integration and transformation solutions, leading large-scale data cleansing initiatives and building end-to-end ETL pipelines that improved data reliability and accessibility for business stakeholders.
Over the years, I have evolved my capabilities to architect and optimize modern, cloud-native data platforms. I have designed scalable data ecosystems leveraging Azure Databricks for distributed data processing, Snowflake for high-performance cloud data warehousing, and DBT for implementing modular, testable, and well-governed transformation frameworks. I have also led the orchestration of enterprise-grade data pipelines using Azure Data Factory and AWS cloud services to support high-volume, multi-source data ingestion and analytics workloads.
From an engineering and analytics perspective, I utilize Python and advanced data processing libraries including Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and TextBlob to develop data enrichment, automation, and advanced analytical solutions. Additionally, I collaborate closely with business and executive stakeholders by delivering insight-driven reporting and interactive dashboards using Power BI and Tableau, enabling strategic decision-making and operational efficiency.
My approach combines strong data architecture principles, governance best practices, and performance optimization strategies to deliver resilient, scalable, and business-aligned data solutions.
